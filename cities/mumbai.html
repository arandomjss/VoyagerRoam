Welcome to "The Gateway of India",Mumbai!!

ATTRACTIONS

Gateway of India

This iconic arch monument, built in the early 20th century, was the ceremonial entrance for British Viceroys arriving in India. Today, it's a popular tourist destination offering stunning views of the Mumbai harbor.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST)

Formerly known as Victoria Terminus, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is an architectural marvel. Built in the Victorian Gothic style, the terminus is adorned with intricate stonework, sculptures, and stained glass windows.

Elephanta Caves

Located on Elephanta Island, a short ferry ride away from Mumbai, these rock-cut caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Dating from the 6th to 7th centuries AD

FEATURES

A Feast for the Senses

Wander through Crawford Market, a kaleidoscope of spices, fresh produce, and local crafts. In the evening, savor the legendary street food of Chowpatty Beach, from piping hot samosas to melt-in-your-mouth pav bhaji.

Architectural Gems

Marvel at the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a stunning example of Victorian Gothic architecture. Seek serenity at the Global Vipassana Pagoda, a magnificent golden dome perfect for meditation.

Gateway to Adventure

Start your day at the iconic Gateway of India, a majestic archway overlooking the Arabian Sea. Embark on a ferry to Elephanta Island, home to ancient cave temples carved from rock.
