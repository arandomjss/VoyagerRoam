Welcome to "The White and Blue City", Mombasa!!

ATTRACTIONS

Fort Jesus Museum

Immerse yourself in Mombasa's rich history at Fort Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, this massive fort served as an important defensive outpost for centuries.

Haller Park

Formerly a barren wasteland turned into a flourishing sanctuary, Haller Park offers a unique opportunity to see wildlife in a rehabilitated ecosystem.

Mombasa Marine National Park and Reserve

Experience the underwater paradise of Mombasa Marine National Park and Reserve, Kenya's oldest marine park. Renowned for its coral reefs teeming with marine life, the park offers excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities.

FEATURES

Relax on Pristine Beaches

Mombasa boasts some of Kenya's most stunning beaches.The crystal-clear turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean are perfect for swimming, snorkeling, or simply soaking up the beauty.

Thrilling Activities

Mombasa offers something for the adventurous soul. Go kitesurfing or windsurfing on the waves, or try deep-sea fishing for a chance to catch a trophy fish.

Wildlife Encounters

Not just a beach destination, Mombasa offers exciting wildlife experiences. Visit Haller Park, a sanctuary dedicated to wildlife conservation. See giraffes, zebras, antelopes, and even crocodiles roaming freely in this unique environment.
