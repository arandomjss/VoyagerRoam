Welcome to "The Harmony City", Tokyo!!

ATTRACTIONS

Sensō-ji Temple

Asakusa's Sensō-ji Temple is one of Tokyo's oldest and most popular temples. Founded in the 7th century, it's dedicated to the bodhisattva Kannon, the goddess of mercy.

Shibuya Crossing

This intersection is known as the "busiest crossing in the world" and is a truly iconic Tokyo experience. Crosswalks from all directions change simultaneously, creating a sea of people walking in every direction.

Tokyo Skytree

Soar above the city from the Tokyo Skytree, the tallest structure in Japan at 634 meters (2,080 ft). Enjoy panoramic views from the observation decks, or visit the restaurants and shops located within the tower.

FEATURES

Electrifying Nightlife

Tokyo's nightlife is legendary, with bustling districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku offering a wide variety of bars, clubs, and karaoke spots.

Cutting-Edge Technology

Tokyo is a global leader in innovation and technology. Visitors can experience the future firsthand by visiting the Miraikan National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation, or by exploring the high-tech district of Akihabara.

Rich History and Culture

Tokyo boasts a long and fascinating history, dating back centuries. Visitors can explore this rich heritage by visiting ancient temples and shrines.
