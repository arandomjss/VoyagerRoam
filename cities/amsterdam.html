Welcome to Amsterdam!!

Canal Ring

Arguably the most iconic feature of Amsterdam, the canal ring is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. A network of 16th and 17th century canals weaving through the city center, it's a picturesque sight to behold.

Rijksmuseum

The Netherlands' national museum, the Rijksmuseum houses an impressive collection of Dutch art from the Middle Ages to the 20th century.

Anne Frank House

A moving and somber experience, the Anne Frank House is the secret annex where Anne Frank and her family hid during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands in World War II.

Cruise Through Canal Wonderland

Amsterdam's web of canals is its crown jewel. Glide through the city on a scenic canal cruise, taking in the picturesque gabled houses lining the waterways.

Indulge Your Inner Foodie

Amsterdam offers a culinary adventure beyond expectations. Sample the city's famous stroopwafel (syrup waffle) with a steaming cup of coffee, or savor fresh herring at a street vendor.

Embrace the Relaxed Amsterdam Lifestyle

Amsterdam is a city that thrives on a slower pace. Rent a bike, the preferred mode of transportation for locals, and explore the city's network of dedicated bike lanes.
