Welcome to "The City of Gold", Dubai!!

ATTRACTIONS

Burj Khalifa

This is the tallest building in the world, at a staggering 828 meters (2,717 feet) tall. You can take an elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

The Dubai Fountain

This choreographed fountain system is located at the base of the Burj Khalifa and is the largest choreographed fountain system in the world.

Dubai Mall

This is one of the largest malls in the world, and it houses over 1,200 stores. The mall also has an aquarium, an ice rink, and a VR park.

FEATURES

Experience the ultimate in luxury

Dubai is home to some of the most luxurious hotels and resorts in the world, as well as world-class shopping malls and restaurants.

Immerse yourself in culture

Explore the city's rich history and heritage by visiting its ancient souks, traditional architecture, and museums.

Be amazed by the architecture

Dubai is home to some of the most iconic and innovative architecture in the world, including the Burj Khalifa, the Palm Jumeirah, and the Dubai Mall.
