Welcome to Paris!!

Eiffel Tower

The undisputed symbol of Paris, the Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower that soars 324 meters (1,063 ft) above the Champ de Mars. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, it was originally intended to be a temporary structure but has become a beloved landmark.

Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most prestigious museums, the Louvre is home to an unparalleled collection of artworks spanning centuries and civilizations.

Musée d'Orsay

Located across the Seine River from the Louvre, the Musée d'Orsay houses an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art from the 19th and early 20th centuries.

Experience the City of Romance

Paris is a city that exudes romance. Stroll hand-in-hand with your loved one along the Champs-Élysées, or whisper sweet nothings beneath the twinkling Eiffel Tower.

A City for All Seasons

Paris is a city that can be enjoyed year-round. Spring brings vibrant blooms to the city's parks, while summer offers the perfect weather for outdoor exploration.

Indulge in Parisian Delights

French cuisine is legendary, and Paris is a paradise for gourmands. Savor flaky croissants in a quaint cafe, or treat yourself to a Michelin-starred dining experience.
